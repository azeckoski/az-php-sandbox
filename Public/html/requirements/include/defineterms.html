Definition of terminology
Vote:
Critical - Cannot use Sakai without it
Essential - Can use Sakai but need this as soon as possible
Desirable - Can use Sakai but would like this
Not Applicable - Does not impact our use of Sakai

Component:
The tools or portions of Sakai that the requirement affects, including "Global" for requirements with a system-wide impact and "New Tool" for required functionality that is best described in the form of a tool to be added to Sakai. 		     Audience:
Students - Students taking classes using Sakai
Instructors - Instructors teaching courses with Sakai
Researchers - Researchers collaborating through Sakai
Staff (Administrative) - Support staff assisting Students, Instructors, and Researchers in using Sakai
Sakai Administrators - System administrators for Sakai
Sakai Developers - Designers, Programmers and QA Specialists working on Sakai
