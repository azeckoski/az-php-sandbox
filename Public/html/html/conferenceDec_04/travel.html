THE CITY

 

W New Orleans is conveniently located near the river, a few blocks away from the lively French Quarter. Eat. Drink. And be merry. Legendary restaurants, incredible jazz and the best of New Orleans's notorious nightlife surround you.

 

HOTEL INFORMATION

 

The Sakai Educational Partner's Program (SEPP) 2004 Winter Conference will be held at the W New Orleans Hotel on December 8 -10, 2004.


For hotel reservations:

Visit the W New Orleans SEPP Conference page or call 1-800-522-6963 to request the Sakai Educational Partners Conference rate of $159/night. (Rate available for Dec. 7-11th). 
  • Check-in is 3:00 p.m.
  • Check-out is 12:00 p.m.
  • Early arrival and late check-out will be provided based on availability
  • Local taxes of 13% and a per night occupancy fee of $2

TRANSPORTATION

 

Louis Armstrong International Airport (MSY) is located 17 miles from the W New Orleans Hotel. The airport is serviced by the following airlines: Air Canada, AirTran, America West, American, Continental, Delta, Frontier, JetBlue, Northwest, Southwest, Taca, United and US Airways.

 

Airport Shuttle: Shuttle service is available from the airport to the hotels for $13 per person, each way. Three bags per person. Call 1-866-596-2699 or 504-522-3500 for to make a reservation, or upon arriving, you can proceed to the counter located on the lower level in the baggage claim area. Advance reservations are required 48 hours prior to travel for all ADA accessible transfers. Please call well enough in advance for the specially-equipped shuttle to be reserved. For group reservations of 10 or more people please dial 1-888-432-7651.

 

Taxicabs: A cab ride costs $28 from the airport to the Central Business District for one or two persons, and $12 per person, for three or more passengers.  Pick-up is on the lower level, outside the baggage claim area.  There may be an additional charge for extra baggage.
