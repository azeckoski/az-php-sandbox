Birds of a Feather

News

Conference attendees are encouraged to join and use the DEC 2004 SEPP Conf discussion group for planning Birds of a Feather (BOF) meetings, discussing other conference related topics, and for sharing resources during the conference.

You can join this group by choosing it from Membership area on your collab.sakaiproject.org account.



Hold your own BOF:

During the June 2004 Sakai conference, attendees arranged BOF (or special interest group) meetings to discuss a variety of Sakai Discussion Group topics and related issues including: user support, installation, workflow and requirements.

We will again be offering a limited amount of meeting space for attendees wanting to take advantage of the face-to-face meeting opportunities.

Please use use the DEC 2004 SEPP Conf discussion group to begin generating interest on various topics and in pre-planning for meetings. Specific details about room availability will be available at the start of the conference.

Check the BOF schedules posted near the registration area, for the most up-to-date information.
